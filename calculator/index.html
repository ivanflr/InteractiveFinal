Our Methodology:

We looked at studies measuring the impact of ACE score on various ailments: migraines, fetal death, depression, cancer, etc. For each illness that appears in our results, we used data from a particular study. These numbers are not predictions. They are based on numbers from other statistically representative populations. All of our data was taken from studies of statistically representative populations that have been published in peer reviewed medical journals since 2001.
